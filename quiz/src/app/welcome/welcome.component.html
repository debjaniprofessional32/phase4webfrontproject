Welcome to quiz app

Quiz contain 10 question & each hold 10 points.

Rules for this quiz:

  1. Correct answer will incur 10 point for each.
  2. Similarly incorrect answer will incur -10 point for each.
  3. Receive 60 second for each question.
  4. Refreshing will reset the quiz(so refrain refresh while doing quiz).
